Soft, buttery cut-out cookies topped with our signature royal icing. A tradition since 1995.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups All-purpose flour
  • 1 cup Unsalted butter
  • 1 cup White sugar
  • 1 Egg + 1 tsp Vanilla
  • Royal Icing & Sprinkles

Method

  1. Cream butter and sugar until fluffy.
  2. Mix in egg and vanilla. Gradually add flour.
  3. Chill dough for 1 hour.
  4. Roll out and cut into shapes.
  5. Bake at 350Â°F for 8-10 minutes. Cool and decorate!

Like a brownie in cookie form. Rolled in powdered sugar before baking for that snowy crackle look.

Method
